About me

Hi there! I'm Harbhajan Singh, a passionate Computer Science engineer & entrepreneur with a Master's degree in Applied Computing specialized in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Windsor.

I'm a 3x Kaggle Expert in Notebooks, Datasets and Discussions - an achievement that reflects my dedication to honing my data science skills and sharing insights with the community. Previously, I worked as a Data Analyst at Tata Consultancy Services, where I had the opportunity to collaborate with talented data scientists on creating innovative solutions. My role allowed me to develop strong leadership abilities while working in a team environment.

My enthusiasm for coding started early and has only grown over time, especially in the domains of data science and data analysis. My professional experiences at companies like Technodata and Artifutech have provided invaluable first-hand insight into how technology can enable businesses. As we witness the expanding integration of business and information technology, I'm committed to effectively bridging the gap between the two by leveraging my expertise.

As a passionate technologist, I live for the moments when complex data transforms into simple but profound insights. My ultimate professional goal is to empower organizations to make better decisions through analytics. When I'm not crunching numbers, you can find me mentoring newbies in data science, advocating for diversity in STEM, and spending time with my family.

I'm always eager to collaborate with passionate professionals and entrepreneurs. Please feel free to connect with me here to exchange ideas!
